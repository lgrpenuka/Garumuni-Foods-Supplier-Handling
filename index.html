Farm to Export

Your Cinnamon's Journey

We give every farmer and supplier complete visibility into how their cinnamon is handled at each stage of our process.

🚜
Collection & Arrival
We collect cinnamon directly from farmers and registered middlemen across Matale, Kurunegala, and Galle districts. Each batch is weighed and tagged with a unique batch ID upon arrival.
Traceability Begins Here
🔍
Quality Inspection
Our graders assess moisture content, quill thickness, colour, and aroma. Each lot is graded against SLS and ISO 6539 Ceylon Cinnamon standards. Rejected lots are returned with full explanation.
Grade: Alba / C5 / Quillings
📦
Inventory & Stock Logging
Accepted batches are digitally logged into our inventory system. Farmers receive an SMS confirmation with their batch ID, quantity received, and grade assigned.
Batch ID Issued
✨
Cleaning & Sorting
Raw cinnamon is carefully cleaned to remove dust, debris, and damaged pieces. Quills are sorted by size and uniformity for consistent packaging quality.
Hygiene Certified
🧪
Laboratory Testing
Independent lab testing for pesticide residues, heavy metals, volatile oil content (minimum 0.7%), and microbial safety. Results shared with farmers on request.
ISO / EU Compliance
🎁
Packaging & Sealing
Export-grade packaging in humidity-controlled conditions. Quills are hand-packed into polypropylene bags or gift boxes per buyer specification, then vacuum sealed.
Export-Ready
🚢
Export & Dispatch
Goods are shipped via Colombo Port with full documentation — Certificate of Origin, Phytosanitary Certificate, and EU Organic Certification where applicable. Farmers can view dispatch status online.
Global Markets Reached
